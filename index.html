About us

Clairvoyance - The Marketing Conclave

Clairvoyance, HPCL's flagship annual marketing event, serves as a vibrant platform for the leadership team to strategize, innovate, and celebrate our achievements. This dynamic two-day conclave features recognition ceremonies, inspiring leadership talks, and interactive presentations that foster alignment with our future goals.

The 2024 edition embraced the theme “Nayi Soch, Naya Josh, Ab Hamari Baari Hai,” highlighting our renewed enthusiasm and unwavering commitment to innovation in the ever-evolving energy landscape.

A standout feature of Clairvoyance is its engaging format, which includes group presentations, virtual panel discussions, and digital walk-throughs that promote collaboration and facilitate learning among participants. The event regularly introduces innovative initiatives, such as our customer outreach campaigns, further strengthening our strategic efforts.

One of the highlights was the Pragati Prangan stalls, which showcased key projects and innovative ideas from various SBUs, giving attendees a hands-on perspective of our progress. Additionally, 13 success stories were featured in a video, celebrating remarkable achievements within HPCL Marketing.

Clairvoyance embodies our shared commitment to growth, innovation, and teamwork, fostering an environment that propels HPCL's future success
