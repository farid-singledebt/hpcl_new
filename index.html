About us

Clairvoyance - The Marketing Conclave

Clairvoyance, HPCL's prestigious annual marketing conclave, serves as a vibrant platform where a vibrant galaxy of stars meet., where current who's who of HPCL and future who's who gather to strategize, innovate, and celebrate their achievements. The two-day event offers a blend of recognition ceremonies, leadership addresses, and interactive presentations designed to inspire, motivate and align our workforce with future business goals.

The 2023 edition of Clairvoyance embraced the theme "Nayi Soch Naya Josh," reflecting a spirit of rejuvenation, innovation, and forward-thinking in navigating the dynamic energy landscape.

One of the highlights of Clairvoyance is its engaging format, including group presentations, panel discussions, and digital walk-throughs that encourage interaction and collaborative learning. The event also regularly features the launch of new initiatives and programs, such as customer outreach campaign. Clairvoyance stands as an emblem of our collective commitment to continuous growth, innovation, and unity, fostering a collaborative environment to drive the company's future success.
